Samsung Galaxy A21s Review

Leonardo Mathot

December 8, 2023

The Samsung Galaxy A21s is an affordable device that offers some great features. A nice Quad-Camera System, a pretty large screen, and long battery life are some of them. There are a few things not so good though, but it could be the perfect option for you if you do not need high performance and your budget is limited.

The best

  • Long Battery Life
  • Good Design
  • Large Screen
  • Quad Camera System

Could be better

  • Poor Quality Display
